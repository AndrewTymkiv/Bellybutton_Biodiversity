Although it may sound repulsive at first, belly buttons may be key to helping us find bacteria with the perfect taste for Improbable Beef. Humans possess many different types of bacteria all around their bodies, but the proteins found in the bacteria of belly buttons may produce a flavour similar to beef. We are testing the bacteria samples of many different people to see if they produce the right taste for our Improbable Beef. Check out some of the results in the interactive charts on this page to learn more.
